Chess is a two-player strategy game played on a checkered gameboard.The gameboard consists of 64 squares arranged in an 8x8 grid. 
Each player starts the game with 16 pieces: one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns.\n")

Following are the rules of chess : 
1) Choose the row and column in which your chess piece is placed.
2) Enter the row first and then column without any space.
3) After that, Available moves and Eliminate chess piece moves will be shown on your screen.
4) Now enter the place where you want to move the chess piece.
5) Again enter the row first and then column without any space.
6) If you enter the right position then press enter and your chess piece will go there.
7) If you enter wrong position then the program shows a message "Enter the Valid Position".
8) After it, you have to enter the correct position.

                 Now you can enjoy the game :)
